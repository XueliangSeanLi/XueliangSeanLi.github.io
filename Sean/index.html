Xueliang Li (Sean)
Feb 23 2023


Hello, I am LI Xueliang (Sean). I am an assistant professor at the School of Design, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).

My research interests include design for mental wellbeing, interaction design of smart wearables, and human-technology relationships. My research is featured in human-computer interaction (HCI) and the interdisciplinary domains between design and health psychology.

Before joining School of Design, SUSTech, I obtained my Ph.D. from the Faculty of Industrial Design Engineering, Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). My PhD thesis focuses on exploring human-wearable relationships in the context of designing smart wearables for people with posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD).
